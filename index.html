About Me

I was born in Cape Town and moved to the Eastern Cape halfway through my school years—a change that taught me early on how to be adaptable and resilient. I’m someone who values hard work, family, and personal growth above all else. I’ve learned that the secret to success is patience and staying positive, even when life gets a bit challenging. I take great pride in being a reliable person who respects the lessons of the past while working hard for the future. For me, it’s all about staying humble, learning something new every day, and making a positive impact on the people around me.

I wasn’t a "bright spark" in school, but once I started working, my curiosity took over. I’ve always wanted to know how things work, so I taught myself the ins and outs of every program I used. I started by creating templates in Word and Excel to make my workload easier, eventually becoming the go-to person for improving productivity in programs like Legal Ease and GhostPractise. I always had a dream of creating my own program, but it wasn't until I started working remotely that I finally took the leap. With my son’s encouragement, I started with Python and recently moved on to Full Stack Web Development. I only started this journey a few months ago, but I’m already hooked and building this personal site to showcase how far a beginner with a bit of grit can go.

Right now, I’m fully focused on mastering Full Stack Development. My goal isn’t just to learn the languages, but to use them to create tools that make life and work simpler for others—just like those first templates did for me. I’m currently building this site as my first major milestone, and I’m excited to see where this new path takes me. Ultimately, my goal is to be commissioned to create custom programs and designs for others, helping them solve their own unique challenges through technology.
